Basic Website Idea - Jeremiah's Personal Portfolio

Purpose

This website will serve as a personal website where I can store examples of previous work experience and to describe myself. This may include blog entries or examples of things I've done in class using different coding languages and/or technologies, as well as presentations or other documents to share my skills.

Audience

This will be primarily for future employers. This will provide an explorable repository of my work and interests to improve the visibility of my skillset. This way, people who may be considering working with me have a tangible representation of my work.
